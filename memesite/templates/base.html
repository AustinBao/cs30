How this website works

A quick introduction

  • Create an account by entering your info on the sign up page (click "SIGN UP" link on top left).
  • Login to that account to gain other features of the website.
  • Immediatly, you can add memes by clicking the "Add meme" button. This should pop up a form.
  • After submitting the meme, it should now display on the home page as a card. You can edit or delete said meme.
  • The "FRIENDS" link will bring you to a page where you can search what memes your friends have added.
  • Finally, "LOGOUT" will log you out of your account and end your session.
